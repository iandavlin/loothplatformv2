You may cancel your subscription at any time. There is no cancellation fee and you do not need to contact a representative to cancel.

  2. Go to Account → Subscription.
  3. Click Cancel subscription.
  4. Confirm cancellation.

Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You will retain access to subscriber features through the end of the period you have already paid for. After that date your account will revert to a free or limited tier (as applicable) and you will no longer be charged.

If you cannot access your account or need help canceling, email ian.davlin@gmail.com and we will process the cancellation within two (2) business days. Once we process the cancellation, no further charges will be made.
