Ian Davlin LLC, doing business as The Looth Group, is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of ability or technology. We actively work to increase the accessibility and usability of loothgroup.com.

Standard We Aim For

We aim to conform our Services to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2, Level AA, published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities.

What We Are Doing

  • Designing pages with semantic HTML and meaningful landmarks;
  • Maintaining sufficient color contrast in text and interactive elements;
  • Providing keyboard navigation for interactive controls;
  • Adding alternative text descriptions for informative images;
  • Including captions or transcripts for video content where available;
  • Testing with screen readers and other assistive technologies during development.

Limitations

Despite our efforts, some content on the Services may not yet be fully accessible. Common limitations include:

  • User-submitted images that lack alternative text;
  • Older posts published before our current accessibility standards were adopted;
  • Third-party tools and embeds (Stripe checkout, Patreon widgets, YouTube embeds) that operate under their own accessibility standards.

We treat known accessibility issues as defects and prioritize them alongside other product work.
